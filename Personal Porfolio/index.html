About

I’m a full stack developer who loves the creativity of front-end design and the technical side of back-end development. When I’m not busy learning code and growing as a developer, I can be found face first in a book or deep in a new crotchet project. Creating new things is my passion, so I’m always excited to delve into a project and bring something fresh and innovative to life. My love of books led me to wanting to write my own stories and I am currently working on a young adult fantasy novel, which I hope to one day get published.
